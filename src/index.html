Understanding the Impact

Student performance is influenced by a complex interplay of academic, lifestyle, and socioeconomic factors. Our analysis reveals that successful students typically demonstrate:

  • Consistent study habits with adequate daily study hours
  • High attendance rates and active class participation
  • Healthy sleep patterns (7-9 hours per night)
  • Balanced lifestyle including physical activity
  • Strong parental support and engagement
  • Access to adequate learning resources
  • High motivation and positive attitude toward learning

By understanding these factors, educators and policymakers can develop targeted interventions to support student success and address performance gaps.
